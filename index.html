2017 November

QRL changed direction from a Proof of Stake model to a Proof of Work mineable algorithm, Cryptonight opening up th possibility for the QRLmining Pool to be created

2018 - February

The QRLmining team was established and built from relationships forged from the mutual interest in cryptocurrency and the knowledge of the present Quantum Computing attack. We started building the pool and related services to help strengthen the QRL network and support the project.

2018 - March

QRL Mining will launch the beta-net mining pool and begin testing the QRL blockchain against a massive hashrate. With the opening of GPU mining capabilities via Pools the available hashrate will more than triple. This pool aims to provide small miners a way to make a profit.

2018 - Q3

The QRL project has planned a hard fork Mid Q3 2018 from a Proof Of Work system to a Proof Of Stake system. This will completely change the way QRL is created and rewarded to the creators. We will move towards other CryptoNight coins to continue gaining from our mining rigs. We will payout all remaining mining rewards on the hard fork date TBD.
