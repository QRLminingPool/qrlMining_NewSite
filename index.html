What Is QRLmining?

2018 - March

QRL Mining will launch the beta-net mining pool and begin testing the QRL blockchain against a massive hashrate. With the opening of GPU mining capabilities via Pools the available hashrate will more than triple.

2018 - February

The QRLmining team was established and built from relationships forged from the mutual interest in cryptocurrency and the knowledge of the present Quantum Computer attack. We started building the pool and related services to help strengthen the QRL network and support the project. We have a strong community foundation based on respect and open source fundamentals.

2017 November

QRL changed direction from a Proof of Stake model to a Proof of Work mineable algorithm, Cryptonight opening up th possibility for the QRLmining Pool to be created
